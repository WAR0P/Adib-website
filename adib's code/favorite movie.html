My Favorite Movie: Howl's Moving Castle

Summary

Howl's Moving Castle is a Japanese animated fantasy film directed by Hayao Miyazaki, based on the novel by Diana Wynne Jones. The story follows Sophie, a young woman transformed into an elderly woman by a witch's curse, who seeks the help of the wizard Howl, who lives in a magical moving castle.
